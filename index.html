ABOUT ME

Actor

From the childhood,I had a dream of becoming an actor but I did not find way to act,thereafter,a theatre group "Samyantar" which was formed by me and Rakesh Verma and and from that group so many plays were staged being directed by different directors, staged at Allahabad and other cities.Prominent amongst them are Dularibai,Rakhtbegh,Trahimam,Poster,Bakri,Kanjoos which made my acting thirst fulfilled to some extend and still I am working with two plays namely Poster and Sar Sar Sarla.


I did roles in so many films but was given oppurtunity to prove my acting skills in ReshamDunkk as Main Villain,which is yet to be released and I also worked as father of main lead heroine,Smriti Sinha,in Hawa Mein Udhta Jaaye Mera Lal Dupatta Mal Mal Ka,directed by Ashok Deep and Naidiya Ke Uss Par directed by Rakesh Srivastava and is staged to be released.An ongoing film,Careless,is still under production.I worked in many albums.A production company named "Sai Nath International" was started by Mrs.Anjana Srivastava under the banner of set production company two albums "Sai Smaran" and "Ma Tera Bulawa Aaya Hai".
